# Poem for Take Your Child to Work Day 2017

Like the buffalo on the plain  
Startled into a run by rain  
Children on this particular day  
Are more ready to play.

Their random movements  
Evocative of quantum moments  
Diagrams of photon activity  
Electrons bouncing avidly.

Again I am a gawky teenager  
In a classroom, none the sager,  
Speaking lines I invented during the drive  
Trying for profundity before I arrive.

Calm\! All this worry is for naught  
All hasty preparations wrought  
For those whom no adults make foes  
And regard their parents as heroes.
