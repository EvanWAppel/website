# I’m Growing Old, Patti Smith

## *To Tifani Tamayo*

Eerie. Does this mean that it's that time  
In life when there are more endings  
Than beginnings? That the rime  
Of age begins to weigh on the living?

We are so god-damned young, yes.  
And the young are certainly god-damned.

*Spring of 2016*
