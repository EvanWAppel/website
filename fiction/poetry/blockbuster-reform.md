# Blockbuster Reform

I wish that some Hollywood producers had the balls  
To make superhero movies with some chutzpah.

Coming this summer, Clark Kent is gunning for a raise,  
But will his neglect of Lois Lane rend them asunder?

Bruce Wayne returns in a psychological blockbuster:  
Agoraphobia: His biggest challenge is leaving the cave.

Chris Hemsworth is Thor, God of actuarial tables\!  
Now your insurance claim is an act of a *Norse* god.

Paul Thomas Anderson presents: Once an Iron Man:  
Tony Stark comes to terms with his bipolarity.

It’s easy to make the extraordinary extraordinary  
And to feature apocalyptic, existential disasters is a breeze,  
But what’s really a trick to accomplish  
Is to make the mundane come alive,  
It’s to make the banal extraordinary,  
To make heroes of the unheroic.
