# Pi Day Song (unfinished)

Gimme a segment of that sweet potato,  
Slice me up a polygon of that pumpkin,  
Don't trouble me with degrees of donuts,  
Because three-point-one-four is Pi Day\!

Care to spare a secant of strawberry?  
Or perhaps another berry? or Cherry?  
Oh, to hell with your cheesy danishes  
Because today, 22 divided by 7 equals Pi Day\!
